Froggy the Frog Privacy Policy

Pwnasaurus Games LLC

Effective date: May 13, 2023

1 Overview

This is the Privacy Policy for Froggy the Frog (“FtF”, “the game”), owned and developed by Pwnasaurus Games LLC (“we”, “us”). We also do business as Pwnasaurus Games.

This Privacy Policy covers

  • What data we collect
  • What we do with your data
  • Your rights

By downloading or playing FtF you acknowledge and consent to this Privacy Policy.

2 Data we collect

We collect the following data you decide to provide us directly.

  • Any comments you send us (including associated contact information)
  • Any scores you choose to publish on the leaderboards

We collect the following data from you automatically while you play FtF.

  • IP Address

  • Device and account identifiers

    • This includes advertising IDs, app set IDs, and other identifiers related to signed-in accounts on the device.

  • Usage data

    • This is data on how you use the game. It includes game launches, taps, and video views.

  • Diagnostic information

    • This is data on the performance of the game and third party SDKs.

We collect the following data about you from third parties.

  • Aggregated statistics related to game downloads, user engagement, and in-game advertisements
  • Leaderboard scores

3 What we do with your data

We have a legitimate business interest to use your data in the following ways.

We use your comments to improve the game.

We use device and account identifiers to enforce the integrity of FtF save files. We do not transfer device and account identifiers off your device when using them for this purpose.

We use certain usage data to allow the game to function. We do not transfer such usage data off your device when using them for this purpose.

We share all categories of automatically collected data with our advertising third party service providers to provide you with personalized advertising (see section 5.1).

We share your User ID for Google Play Games Services (on Android) and Game Center (on iOS), usage data, diagnostics information, and any scores you choose to publish on the leaderboards with our other third party service providers to provide you with leaderboards (see section 5.2).

We use data about you from third parties to manage our business and improve the game.

4 Your rights

You can opt out of personalized advertising by selecting the “Opt out of Ads Personalization” setting on Android (Settings → Google → Ads) or by revoking permission for FtF to track you on iOS (Settings → Privacy → Tracking).

You can reset your advertising ID by selecting the “Reset advertising ID” option on Android (Settings → Google → Ads). If you have Android 12 you can delete your advertising ID in the same menu.

You can manage your Google Play Games Services data and privacy settings using the Google Play Games app on Android. You can manage your Game Center privacy settings on iOS (Settings → Game Center). You will be signed-in to these services by default when playing the game, but they are optional. You can avoid using them by signing out prior to starting the game and/or declining the sign-in prompt, but you will not have access to leaderboards.

You can contact us to (see section 12)

  • Request a copy of your data in electronic format
  • Request that your data be deleted
  • Object to how we use your data
  • Request that we restrict processing of your data
  • Correct or update your data

You will not face discriminatory treatment by exercising any of your rights.

In order to protect our data, we may ask you to verify your identity if you contact us to exercise your rights.

9 Children

FtF does not address children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 16 years of age. If you are under 16 years of age, do not play FtF. If you are under 16 years of age and you have played FtF or if your child or ward of less than 16 years of age has played FtF, contact us (see section 12) to have the minor’s data deleted.
